Privacy Policy

Outlining how we handle data for our enterprise partners and public sector stakeholders.

1. Introduction 2. Data Collection 3. Data Processing 4. How We Use Data

1. Introduction

Innovation2Solution Limited respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle personal information collected via our website and during technical audits.

2. Data Collection

We collect information strictly necessary for the execution of digital transformation audits, including corporate contact details and infrastructure metadata.

3. Data Processing

We process data based on legitimate business interests and the fulfillment of requested services. This includes:

  • Inquiry Management: Processing names and corporate emails provided via our contact forms to respond to service requests and support inquiries.
  • Technical Auditing: Analyzing submitted infrastructure data to provide tailored Solution Architecture recommendations.
  • Service Delivery: Using client information to manage the lifecycle of Agentic AI deployments and Managed Transformations.
  • Security & Compliance: Monitoring site interactions to prevent fraudulent activity and ensure data sovereignty standards are met.

4. How We Use Data

Innovation2Solution uses your data to provide, maintain, and improve our services. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Data is shared only with vetted sub-processors essential for service delivery, such as secure hosting providers and CRM tools, all of which comply with UK GDPR standards.

About Us

Expert consultants. Real-world experience. No nonsense.

Innovation 2 Solution was founded on a simple belief: that organisations of every size deserve access to expert, independent digital consultancy — without the overhead and jargon that comes with the large system integrators. We are a lean, experienced team that punches well above our weight.

Who We Are

A specialist team with deep, cross-sector experience

Founded in 2015 and registered at Company No. 09476703, Innovation 2 Solution is headquartered in Edgbaston, Birmingham — one of the UK's most dynamic tech and business hubs — with clients across the UK, Caribbean, and beyond.

We are practitioners, not theorists. Every member of our consulting team has delivered real programmes — in the public sector, in enterprise, in start-ups — and brings that hands-on experience to every client engagement. We don't deploy junior analysts and oversee from a distance. Senior consultants do the work.

  • 15+ years of combined delivery experience across the team
  • Engagements across 3 continents — UK, Caribbean, North America
  • Public sector, SME, enterprise and start-up experience
  • Microsoft, ITIL, PRINCE2, Cisco, CompTIA and Agile certified

Our Values

What drives every engagement

Integrity Above All

We tell clients what they need to hear, not what they want to hear. If a proposed solution isn't the right one, we'll say so — even if it means a smaller project for us.

Outcomes, Not Outputs

We're accountable for the benefits our work creates — not just the deliverables. We measure success by what changes in your organisation, not what we hand over.

Partnership Thinking

We don't want to be a supplier you transact with once. We build long-term relationships that grow as your organisation grows — because our best work comes from deep understanding.

Who We Serve

Deep expertise across sectors — adapted to your context

Digital transformation looks different in a government department, a growing SME, and a funded startup. We bring the right approach, the right experience, and the right language to every type of organisation we work with.

Public Sector

Digital transformation within public sector constraints

We understand the complexity of public sector digital — the procurement rules, the approval cycles, the stakeholder politics, the compliance requirements, and the accountability to the public. We've worked with central government, local authorities, NHS-adjacent organisations, and internationally with multilateral development bodies.

Our team holds the certifications and has the experience to navigate GDS standards, ITIL-compliant service management, and the unique challenge of delivering change in organisations where adoption is mandated but hearts and minds must still be won.

  • Central government and arms-length bodies
  • Local authority digital transformation
  • Education sector — schools, colleges, universities
  • International development and NGO digital programmes

SME & Growth Businesses

Enterprise-grade thinking at a size that makes sense

Growing businesses face a unique challenge: they need technology that can scale with them, but don't yet have the budget, internal capability, or risk appetite of a large enterprise. We bridge that gap — bringing strategic thinking and quality delivery calibrated to your stage of growth.

Whether you need a technology roadmap, a bespoke application, or a data platform, we design solutions that grow with you — without overbuilding for a scale you haven't yet reached.

  • Technology strategy for scaling businesses (10–250 staff)
  • Operational automation to improve margins
  • Customer-facing digital products and portals
  • Data and analytics for commercial decision-making

Our Process

How we work with you

01

Discovery & Requirements

We run structured workshops with your stakeholders and end users to define requirements, map user journeys, and agree the minimum viable product scope. Everything documented and signed off.

02

UX Design & Prototyping

Before a line of code is written, we design the user experience. Wireframes, high-fidelity prototypes, and usability testing. You see exactly what you're getting before build begins.

03

Agile Development & Testing

We build iteratively in two-week sprints with regular demos. Automated testing, security scanning, and performance testing built into every sprint — not bolted on at the end.

04

Launch, Training & Support

We manage deployment, provide user documentation and training, and offer ongoing support and feature development through a flexible retainer or project-based model.
